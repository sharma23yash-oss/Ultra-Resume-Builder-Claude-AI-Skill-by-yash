## Certification Injection Rules

### In CERTIFICATIONS Section (already held)
```
Format: [Full Name] | [Issuer] | [Year] | [License # if applicable]
Example: NISM Series VIII: Equity Derivatives | NISM (SEBI) | 2024 | XXXXXX
```

### At Bottom of Resume (recommended/in-progress)
```
Format: [Name] ([Status]) | [Issuer] — [1 line JD relevance]
Example: Bloomberg Market Concepts (Enrolled) | Bloomberg — Covers
         terminal skills required for equity research role at [Company]
```

### ATS Optimization Rule
Always spell out acronyms ONCE:
- "CFA (Chartered Financial Analyst)" not just "CFA"
- "NISM (National Institute of Securities Markets)" on first mention
- "JAIIB (Junior Associate of the Indian Institute of Bankers)" not just "JAIIB"

### Priority Selection Logic
```python
# When recommending certifications:
1. Is the certification explicitly mentioned in JD? → MUST INCLUDE
2. Is the issuer well-known in the industry? → High priority
3. Is it free or low cost (<₹3,000)? → Prefer it
4. Can it be completed before application deadline? → Include
5. Does it cover a gap identified in the gap map? → High priority
```
